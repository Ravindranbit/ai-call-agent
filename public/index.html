Capabilities

Built for Real-World Complexity

Traditional systems fail on accents, dialects, and high-stress situations. 1092 Assist is designed to listen, confirm, and care.

Multilingual & Dialect Aware

Native support for Kannada, Hindi, Tamil, and English. Fine-tuned to recognize local Karnataka dialects and colloquialisms.

Verification Loop

Eliminates AI hallucinations by restating the citizen's problem and asking for confirmation before triggering any government action.

Emotion & Urgency

Real-time sentiment analysis detects distress, anger, or fear, automatically flagging high-urgency medical or fire emergencies.

Human-in-the-Loop

Seamless escalation to human agents when confidence drops or distress is high, ensuring no citizen is left unheard.

Zero Hallucination

Strictly bounded by a verified Government Knowledge Base. The AI only answers what it knows to be true.

Real-time Ops Panel

A premium administrative dashboard for live call monitoring, transcript reviews, and instant AI behavior tuning.
